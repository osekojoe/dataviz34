Visual Design Approach

Our visual design strategy involved a structured process of exploration, comparison, and refinement. We created a variety of sketches tailored to address specific research questions, gradually refining our visualizations to effectively communicate findings and insights.

By balancing complexity and simplicity, we developed a series of final sketches that provide clear, interpretable representations of the data.

Reworked Sketches

  • Geographical Influence on Product Demand: This sketch illustrates the flow of products and order characteristics across different regions, emphasizing D.E.A.D.'s core business areas and product diversity within orders.
  • Delivery Time per Region: This sketch explores delivery times across regions, using bar lengths to represent average delivery durations and widths to depict order volumes.
  • Trend of Product Demand Over Five Years: This sketch visualizes the evolution of product demand over five years, facilitating comparisons between product types and years through unique color-coded nodes.
