Summary

This project report focuses on the extensive sales data of Dumbledilp's Equipment & Adventuring Distributions Inc. (D.E.A.D.), a prominent e-commerce distributor of adventuring equipment worldwide.

Over the past five years, D.E.A.D. has meticulously recorded sales, customer details, product information, and service regions. The objective of this project is to leverage this wealth of data to identify sales trends, analyze delivery efficiency, assess product performance, and understand customer confidence within the Forgotten Realms.

In conclusion, this report showcases the culmination of our data visualization efforts, offering valuable insights into D.E.A.D.'s operational dynamics and market trends within the adventuring equipment industry. The following sections detail our findings and interpretations through the lens of the presented visualizations.

Project Description

D.E.A.D. has compiled detailed records of its operations, including customer demographics, product types, regions served, and order timelines.

Our analysis aims to uncover geographical influences on product demand, evaluate delivery times across different regions, and examine shifts in market demand over a five-year period based on product types and subtypes.

Research Questions

  1. What is the impact of geographical regions on product demand and order characteristics?
  2. How does delivery time vary across different regions, and what factors influence these variations?
